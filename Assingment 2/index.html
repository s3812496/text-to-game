Estimation Timetable

Our Team “Text-To-Game” estimates that we will collectively need to have worked at least 80 hours once we’ve finished. Most of this time will be spent developing our lines of code for each of our features. This information is based off our own self evaluations on how efficiently we each performed in previous programming assignments. However, since a few members of the group are still learning the basics to java, we expect that they will take longer than the rest of the group and estimated the times accordingly. Due to the nature of programming, there will be some cross-over where some members may code small parts of other people’s sections in order to join everything together without producing syntax errors. We plan on having at least one meeting with our mentor Ivan Sun to gather feedback on our work which will add an additional 20-30 minutes.
