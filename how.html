Group Contract

The expectations of all group members regarding group meeting are as follows:

  • All members must attend a meeting at least once a week
  • All members are expected to contribute during the meetings
  • All members are expected to assign themselves tasks to complete during the week

The process for dealing with unmet expectations or problems that may arise:

  1. The leader assigned to the area will be informed by the person experiencing the difficulty’s
  2. The leader will attempt to resolve the issue by themselves
  3. If the problem still persists a group meeting will be held to resolve the issue

Peer feedback will be dealt with in the following manner:

  1. The group leader is to be informed of the feedback
  2. The group leader will bring this forward with the person in question
  3. If this person continues to not rectify the problem a group meeting will be held
  4. If the problem still persists the tutor will be contacted regarding it

The Trello board is to be updated after items are completed and is the tool that will be used to track progress and contributions to the project, GitHub will also be used as we enter the programming stages of the project changes are only to be pushed if they have a valid description to them.

Group members are to indicate their agreement regarding this contract on Spark PLUS.
